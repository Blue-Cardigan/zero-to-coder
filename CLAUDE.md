## Design Context

### Users
Learners joining "Zero to Coder" workshops, often at beginner level, and visitors deciding whether to attend.
They need confidence in the instructor, clarity on workshop value, and quick access to upcoming sessions and outcomes.

### Brand Personality
Practical, energetic, and credible.
The interface should feel like a guided build session: clear structure, modern energy, and high trust.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial technical dark theme based on the slide deck system (`ztc` tokens and surfaces), with sharp panel geometry, restrained gradients, and strong content hierarchy.
Avoid generic glowing-card aesthetics; prioritize disciplined composition and purposeful accents.

### Design Principles
- Reuse shared visual tokens across slides and main site to keep one coherent brand language.
- Prioritize clarity and hierarchy first; decorative treatment should support comprehension.
- Prefer distinctive panel archetypes (`hero-panel`, `info-panel`, `cta-panel`) over generic card stacks.
- Keep contrast high and text readable across all viewport sizes.
- Use motion sparingly to reinforce meaning, not to decorate.
